ARISE: Online/Offline Education Portal




Platform Overview

ARISE is an innovative online/offline education portal dedicated to promoting civic sense and values. Our platform caters to students, teachers, parents, and the general public, providing a diverse range of instructional content to cultivate a sense of accountability and responsibility for sustainable living.


Key Features

  • Multifaceted Content: ARISE offers a plethora of content in various formats, including text, videos, case studies, and activity modules, ensuring engagement and relevance across diverse learning styles.
  • Praxis Model: Our praxis model integrates theoretical knowledge with practical application, encompassing experiences, instructional models, case studies, good practices, and activity modules to facilitate experiential learning.

Vision Statement

ARISE envisions a society where individuals are empowered with the knowledge, skills, and values to make informed decisions, uphold democratic principles, and actively contribute to the creation of a sustainable and accountable future.
